Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How can I search for hotels on your website?

You can find a search bar at the top of our website with different search filters you can choose from.

What information is provided for each hotel listing?

SuiteSpot provides the price, location, and a short description for each hotel listing.

Can I book a room directly through your website?

With SuiteSpot, you are able to book a room directly through our website.

Can I book additional services or amenities through your website?

Unfortunately, at the moment SuiteSpot does not provide additional services or amenities.

Pricing, Billing, & Support

What payment methods are accepted?

At SuiteSpot, we accept payments from all major credit card providers, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows for free cancellations up to 48 hours before the scheduled check-in time. Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in will result in a charge equal to one night's stay.

For refunds, any fees paid at the time of booking (excluding taxes and service charges) will be refunded in full for cancellations made at least 48 hours prior to check-in.

How can I contact customer support if I have a question or issue?

To contact customer support, you can go to our "Contact Us" page and fill out a short form with your question or issue.
